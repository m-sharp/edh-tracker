## Problem

The deck settings tab feels cramped — all content is pushed to the left side of the screen with no breathing room or visual balance. On wider screens this looks especially awkward.

## Solution

Revisit the layout of the settings tab in DeckView. Consider centering content, using a max-width container, adding appropriate spacing, or organizing fields into a more structured grid/form layout consistent with the rest of the app.
